Training Mode

Welcome to the training mode! In this mode, the app will not request an emotion at all. Instead, you are free to take any expression that you like and either take a picture using your webcam or upload existing ones. The AI classifier will then take this pictures and tell you what emotion it thought you were trying to display and what the accuracy was.
